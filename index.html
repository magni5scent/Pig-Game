Players take turns in rolling the dice if you roll a 1 - your score resets to 0 and switches to the next player. The first player to score greater or equal to 100 wins the game.

Rolling for a maximum of 3 turns is considered the safest strategy to avoid rolling a 1. Accumulating your point while holding is a good move on the long run.😉
